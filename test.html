In the spirit of Rotaract’s dedication to service, the initiative, “Honour the Hidden Heroes,” shines a light on those often-overlooked champions who tirelessly contribute to our society from the shadows. For October, our heartfelt gratitude extends to the unsung heroes: Ambulance drivers.

On the 13th October, we had the privilege of donating Morning meal-breakfast to the hardworking ambulance drivers in sunshine hospitals. Yet, the most priceless part was spending time with them, engaging in conversations that revealed their remarkable stories and witnessing their faces light up with happiness.

The Rotaract Club of GNITS remains steadfast in working towards Rotaract’s overarching principles - taking action through service and bringing about positive change to revolutionize communities.
